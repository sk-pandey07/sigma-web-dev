About spider-man

Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is one of the most iconic superheroes in the world. He gained his powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider, which gave him abilities like wall-climbing, super strength, agility, and spider-sense.

He protects New York City from dangerous villains while balancing his personal life. His journey is guided by the famous quote, "With great power comes great responsibility."

Spider-Man Villains

Spider-Man faces some of the most dangerous and iconic villains in Marvel history.

Green Goblin (Norman Osborn)

A genius businessman turned into a dangerous villain. Uses advanced technology and a glider to attack Spider-Man.

Doctor Octopus (Otto Octavius)

A brilliant scientist with four powerful mechanical arms. One of Spider-Man’s smartest enemies.

Venom

An alien symbiote bonded with Eddie Brock. Stronger than Spider-Man and immune to his spider-sense.

Sandman

Can turn his body into sand and reshape it into powerful weapons. Nearly impossible to defeat physically.

Lizard (Dr. Curt Connors)

A scientist who transforms into a reptilian monster. Combines intelligence with brute strength.
