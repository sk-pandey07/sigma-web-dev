petter parker (spiderman)

Spider-Man ek popular superhero hai jo Marvel Comics ka character hai. Iska real naam Peter Parker hai. Woh ek ordinary student tha, lekin ek radioactive spider ke bite ke baad uske paas special powers aa gayi—jaise wall pe chadhne ki ability, super strength, aur spider-sense (danger feel karna). Spider-Man apni web shooters ki help se buildings ke beech swing karta hai aur criminals se fight karta hai. Uska famous dialogue hai: “With great power comes great responsibility.”
